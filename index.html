Analysed the inventory data for one of the largest food and drug retail American company to identify and reduce loss in grocery chain by 8%. Techologies used:

Maintenance and route optimization

Worked with one of the largest American petroleum company's business team to identify business rules given the historical and GIS (Geographic Information Systems) data to optimize route and pending orders. Worked with one of the contruction companies from London to develop a webapp. The objective was to analyse two different types of documents: Risks involved and mitigation of risks to determine the quality of mitigation documents.
